Coffee House Scheduler

Overview:

KOCs (Keepers of Coffee, AKA baristas) are scheduled at the beginning of each semester to work certain shifts every week. A google survey is sent out to everyone to fill out their availability for the semester as well as other relevant information such as who they prefer to work with. All information is kept and stored in multiple different google worksheets.
