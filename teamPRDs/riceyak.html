RiceYak

Overview:

We are an anonymous messaging platform dedicated to fostering university-wide culture and providing a user-friendly alternative to popular communication venues such as GroupMe. Students can use us as both a tool/source of entertainment by enjoying a cleaner interface for university-wide interactions.
